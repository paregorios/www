URI for this person: http://paregorios.org/demarc/people/sempronius-l

Name: Sempronius, L.

Description: Proconsul of Creta et Cyrenae in AD 191/192 or AD 192/193. He has been provisionally identified with a proconsul of Asia (ca. AD 209), whose name is partially preserved in a boundary-related inscription ([ --- Sem]pronio Senecione [proconsule ---]).
