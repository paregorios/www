Pomponius Rufus, Q.

URI for this person: http://paregorios.org/demarc/people/pomponius-rufus-q

Name: Pomponius Rufus, Q.

Description: Propraetorian imperial legate in the province of Hispania Tarraconensis (most probably a legatus iuridicus), under Vespasian. He may be the Rufus named on curse tablets from the area of Emporion, but see L. Novius Rufus.
