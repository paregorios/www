Terentius Gentianus, D.

URI for this person: http://paregorios.org/demarc/people/terentius-gentianus-d

Name: Terentius Gentianus, D.

Description: Although there has been much debate on the part of modern scholars as to what sort of imperial legate he was when he carried out a boundary demarcation in Macedonia, it seems most likely that he was there directing a census during the reign of Hadrian (he is named cens(itor) prov(inciae) Mac(edoniae) in an honorific inscription: CIL 2.1463 = ILS 1046).
