Petronius Celer, C.

URI for this person: http://paregorios.org/demarc/people/petronius-celer-c

Name: Petronius Celer, C.

Description: Procuratorial governor of Mauretania Caesariensis in AD 137. Also seems to have acted in procuratorial capacity in Mauretania Sitifensis where he assigned boundaries to the Numidae, ex indulgentia imperatoris.
