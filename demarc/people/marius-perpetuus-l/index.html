Marius Perpetuus, L.

URI for this person: http://paregorios.org/demarc/people/marius-perpetuus-l

Name: Marius Perpetuus, L.

Description: An individual whose name appears on a fragmentary inscription from Aunobari (Text 41.2) which may record the members of a governor’s consilium as part of a boundary dispute ruling (see Instance 41). He is further described thereon as scriba quaestorius and, although he does not seem to have merited his own entry in PIR2, he is thought to have been the father of L. Marius Perpetuus, L. filius (PIR2 M313). The younger Perpetuus’ equestrian career under the emperor Marcus Aurelius is well-documented and included numerous procuratorial posts and a minor pontificate.
