Name: [Nonius] Asprenas C[ --- ]anus, [P.]

Description: Individual who settled a boundary dispute in Cilicia, acting either as govenor or as a iudex datus of the governor. He may be identifiable with one of two known Nonii who flourished under Vespasian and Domitian.
