Clodius Celsus

URI for this person: http://paregorios.org/demarc/people/clodius-celsus

Name: Clodius Celsus

Description: An otherwise unknown legate who assisted the proconsul of Asia in implementing a demarcation of the sacred lands of Artemis at Ephesus in accordance with an edict of the emperor Domitian sometime between AD 84 and 87.
